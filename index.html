Who even am I?

Hi! As you could probably figure out already, I have a great interest in pixel-art. Why? Hard to say, something about it just calls to me.

This website was originally crated as a way to collect everything I make neatly in one place with a nice gallery.

And while I might be inconsistent with my posts, I make sure to keep this website up do date.

Is that all I should expect?

No, not really. As time went on I decided to expand this place and include some written post and dump some of my old poems.

I am also working on few javascript based projects that I will include here once they are finished as well as some web based games.

And that should make me stay?

That really is only up to you. Free will and all that shit you know.

But as for my opinion: sure. After all, why not? It is what it is, but so is all else.
